# Privacy & Transparency Policy

We believe in keeping things simple. This site is a community resource built to help people follow the Standards Not Force Initiative. Here is how we handle your data.


This policy ONLY applies to the standardsnotforce.org website. Clicking links to other websites could mean that different privacy policies and data practices apply.

---

### 1. Data & Analytics
We value your privacy as much as our own.
* **Posthog Analytics:** We use [Posthog](https://posthog.com/) to see how many people visit the site, troubleshoot errors, and improve our site so it can reach more people more effectively.

### 2.  Opting Out

We configure the site analytics specifically to respect privacy signals like legacy DNT (Do Not Track) and GPC (Global Privacy Control). To opt out, ensure your browser sends at least one of these signals. You can test this by visiting https://globalprivacycontrol.org/ and looking for the banner at the top of the screen.

Web developers can also check the website console/logs in the developer tools for a logged "Privacy signal detected. PostHog tracking disabled" message to confirm you are being opted out.

### 3. External Links
We provide links to third-party websites. Once you click a link and leave our site, we have no control over the content or privacy practices of those websites.
